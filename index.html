OddOnes

A clean visual puzzle game with replayable levels. The homepage stays simple, each level refreshes automatically when you play again, and the first four game modes each contain five rounds.

Choose your challenge

The first three levels sit across the top, with the last two centred neatly underneath.

  • Less punishing maths
